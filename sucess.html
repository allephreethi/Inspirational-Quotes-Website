50 Inspiring Quotes About Success!

What is success?
There are many definitions, but there's one thing all the greats agree on:
Success only comes by persevering despite failure.

Sucess Quotes!
  1. "Success is not final; failure is not fatal: It is the courage to continue that counts."
    — Winston S. Churchill
  2. "The first step toward success is taken when you refuse to be a captive of the
    environment in which you first find yourself."
    – Mark Caine
  3. "It’s not whether you get knocked down, it’s whether you get up."
    – Vince Lombardi
  4. "The question isn’t who is going to let me, it’s who is going to stop me."
    – Ayn Rand
